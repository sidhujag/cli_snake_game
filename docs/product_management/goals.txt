Product Goals:
1. Create an engaging command-line version of the snake game that is easy to start and play, with a focus on smooth and responsive controls.
2. Design a simple and intuitive user interface that displays real-time game information, such as the score and game status, to enhance player engagement.
3. Implement persistent game features, including high score tracking and the ability to pause and resume, to provide a complete gaming experience.
